Donation Process

STEP 1

Register and login as a blood donor.

STEP 2

Search for your residing city and you will be given some available donation center at your city

STEP 3

Select one of them of your own choice , you will be finally get your unique code

STEP 4

Visit the donation center and show them your unique code , once physical checkup will be done , they will take your blood and your donation status will get Approved

FINISH

Finally you can rate your experience
